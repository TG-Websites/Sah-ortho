OUR TECHNOLOGY

PRECISION ENGINEERED FOR PERFORMANCE

At SAH Ortho, we combine advanced engineering, high-grade materials, and strict quality control to manufacture world-class orthopaedic implants. Our implants are CNC-machined from certified stainless steel and titanium alloys, ensuring optimal strength, biocompatibility, and longevity.

Each product undergoes rigorous mechanical testing and is manufactured in ISO 13485 certified facilities. From anatomical plate contouring to variable-angle locking systems, every innovation is designed to simplify surgery and improve patient outcomes.

VARIABLE ANGLE LOCKING

Our plates feature advanced Variable Angle (VA) locking technology, enabling precise screw placement within a 30° cone of freedom. This flexibility ensures optimal fixation even in challenging anatomical regions.

HYBRID FIXATION OPTIONS

Each hole accommodates both locking and non-locking screws, allowing surgeons to tailor fixation strategies to individual fracture patterns without changing instrumentation.

SECURE DRILLING INTERFACE

Precision-engineered drill guides provide a firm “click-fit” engagement with the plate, minimizing skiving and ensuring accuracy during screw insertion.
