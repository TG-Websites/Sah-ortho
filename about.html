ABOUT SAH ORTHO
Trusted Innovation in Orthopaedic Implants

SAH ORTHO – EXCELLENCE IN ORTHOPAEDIC IMPLANTS

SAH Ortho is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of high-quality orthopaedic implants and surgical instruments. With a strong focus on innovation, precision, and patient safety, we specialize in trauma solutions ranging from locking plates, nails, screws, and external fixation systems to advanced surgical instruments.
Certified with ISO and CE , our products are crafted from premium-grade Stainless Steel and Titanium to ensure durability, biocompatibility, and reliability for orthopaedic surgeons worldwide.

SERVING SURGEONS ACROSS
INDIA & BEYOND

Based in North West Delhi, India, SAH Ortho caters to hospitals, distributors, and surgeons across India and exports to multiple countries. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge orthopaedic trauma solutions backed by strong technical expertise and responsive customer support.

OUR PRODUCT RANGE

From advanced locking plates like Femoral Distal, Clavicle Hook, and Proximal Femur LCP Plates, to nails, screws, and external fixation systems, SAH Ortho delivers a complete portfolio for orthopaedic trauma care. We also provide comprehensive instrument sets for surgical convenience, trusted by orthopaedic specialists nationwide.
