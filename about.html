THE SAH ORTHO
STORY

MADE IN SWITZERLAND

The name Bonebridge stands for what we do: We bridge broken bones with advanced implants. As a small company rooted in Switzerland, we embody the pioneering spirit of the Swiss AO founders, who revolutionized fracture treatment with their innovative techniques in the 1960s.
Our Swiss DNA drives our commitment to excellence in orthopaedic trauma: Our implants are developed and manufactured in Switzerland with the attention to detail, diligence, and precision our country is renowned for.

SERVING CUSTOMERS ON THREE
CONTINENTS

Originally a start-up with two employees, we are now a small company with more than 20 employees at our headquarters in Switzerland as well as our subsidiaries in the USA and Australia/New Zealand. As our international reach increases, we strive to serve our customers with the same dedication and passion as we did in our very beginnings.

THE BRIDGES OF
BONEBRIDGE

To reflect and honor our country‘s long-standing tradition in the field of orthopaedic trauma, we decided early on to name our products after Swiss bridges. In 2019 we launched our first product: the SALGINA 2.5mm Distal Radius Plate, named after the Salginatobel Bridge in Schiers in the canton of Grisons, Switzerland.
