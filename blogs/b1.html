Tips for Healthy Bones

Published on Aug 20, 2025 | By SAH Ortho

Maintaining healthy bones is essential for mobility, strength, and long-term health. By understanding key lifestyle factors, you can reduce the risk of fractures and osteoporosis.

1. Balanced Nutrition

Ensure a diet rich in calcium, vitamin D, and protein. Dairy products, leafy greens, nuts, and fortified foods support bone density.

2. Regular Exercise

Weight-bearing exercises like walking, jogging, and resistance training help strengthen bones and improve joint stability.

3. Avoid Harmful Habits

  • Limit alcohol consumption.
  • Avoid smoking, as it weakens bone structure.

4. Bone Health Screenings

Regular check-ups and bone density tests can detect early signs of osteoporosis and help you take preventive measures.

By following these tips, you can maintain strong, healthy bones and enjoy a more active lifestyle at every age.
