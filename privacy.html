Privacy Policy

Your Privacy, Our Commitment

1. Introduction

At SAH Ortho, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, interact with our products, or communicate with us.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Details

Name, email address, phone number, company details.

Usage Data

IP address, browser type, device details, pages visited, and time spent.

Transactional Data

Order history, invoices, shipping address (if applicable).

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, improve, and customize our products and services.
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests.
  • Send important updates about new products, offers, or policy changes.
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure servers, restricted access) to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., payment gateways, shipping partners).
  • Regulatory authorities if required by law.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies to improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of the data we store about you.
