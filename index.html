ORTHOPAEDIC TRAUMA – SAH ORTHO

At SAH Ortho, we are committed to delivering advanced orthopaedic trauma solutions with uncompromised quality. Our extensive product portfolio – including screws, plates, wires, instrument sets, and precision instruments – is developed to address complex clinical needs with simplicity and efficiency. By focusing on essential requirements, we ensure long-term value for patients, surgeons, and healthcare providers worldwide.

SIMPLICITY IS OUR STRENGTH

  • A comprehensive yet efficient range of orthopaedic implants including cortical screws, locking plates, LCP/DCP plates, nails, and specialized instruments.
  • Instrument sets engineered for multi-purpose use – reducing inventory needs while covering a wide range of orthopaedic procedures.
  • Designed and developed in close collaboration with experienced surgeons to meet real-world surgical challenges.
  • Streamlined manufacturing and quality control processes ensuring ISO and CE certified excellence.
