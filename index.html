THE CONCEPT OF
REDUCING
COMPLEXITY

REDUCING COMPLEXITY IN
ORTHOPAEDIC TRAUMA

We create added value by consistently focusing on the essentials. By carefully analyzing existing clinical problems, we develop high-quality solutions that create lasting value for patients, doctors and hospital staff through their simplicity. Reducing complexity is our true innovation.

LESS IS MORE

  • Our rapidly growing implant portfolio covers an ever-increasing range of fractures of the human skeleton. At the same time, the implant portfolio remains efficient.
  • Our instruments are designed in such a way that a maximum number of different indications can be covered with a minimum number of instruments.
  • As a physician-led company, we are and remain close to clinical practice and work closely with clinicians and hospital staff.
  • We keep our processes within our company lean.

WHY THIS IS SO IMPORTANT TODAY

1

USER-FRIENDLY HANDLING

  • The implants and instruments are kept simple and designed for efficient and intuitive handling
  • Only two instrument sets allow the use of the complete product range
2

HIGH EFFICIENCY

  • A small number of instruments and implants allow for quick setup in the OR
  • The use of the same instruments for different fracture treatments allows for:
    • a higher turnover of the individual instruments
    • a faster learning curve for surgeons in training
    • a lower susceptibility to errors
3

OPTIMIZED ALLOCATION OF RESOURCES

  • The streamlined implant portfolio leads to a smaller stock in the hospital and complication-free consignment stock
  • Intelligent instruments for any application allow for:
    • cost-efficient cleaning and sterilisation
    • fewer manual errors due to high turnover
    • a reduction in sterilization costs
